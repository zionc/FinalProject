World Cup Info
- By hitting the commence button you simulate a regualr sean which is here are 
  teams play the other in their league once.
- The teams with highest ranks from their league go to the world cup
- The first round of the world cup is teams are grouped with a seed team and 
  games are played in a round robin format. team with most points advances
- The next round is all knockout rounds and teams progress as they win or lose. Games cvan be won in  
  overtime, by penalty kicks, or a tie.
- The final two teams play each other for the championship while third and fourth places play each 
  other for the third spot.
